Natalie Bell - 'Fairytale Ending' Music Video

 

< / title >

< / head >

< body >

< div class ="

THE BRIEF

">

< p >

As a way to introduce us to the program, After Effects, we were required to create a music video for a South African Artist. My chosen artist was Natalie Bell, a 20-year-old singer-songwriter from Durban. The song I chose was her single, 'Fairytale Ending'. 'Fairytale Ending' tells the story of a frustrated love. The speaker is unable to move on from a man that doesn't love her the way she loves him.

< / p >

< / div >

< div class ="

INSIGHTS & EXECUTION

">

< p >

The key emotions that I identified in the song were frustration that transformed into a determined hope. The frustation is born out of the lack of communication between the speaker and the man she loves, yet she comes to hope for more from love.

< / p >



< p >

My concept is 'desperate communication'. Throughout the song various forms of communication are used; from simple text messages to attention-demanding banners behind planes. All the visuals are examples of methods used by the woman in an attempt to grab the attention of the man in the song.

< / p >



< p >

I used a soft palett accompanied by a flat design style to match the sweet sound of the song. In the end the song is about boldly refusing to give up on love.

< / p >
