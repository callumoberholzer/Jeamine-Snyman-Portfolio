THE BRIEF

">

< p >

Annually Vega Students participate in 'Brand Challenges' which is an opportunity Vega provides to its students to work on "Live Briefs". In other words the students design a campaign or marketing strategy for real clients. In the first week of Brand Challenges each team of students is required to create an 'agency' which they will belong to for the duration of Brand Challenges. This brand identity includes the values, personality and strategic identity of our team/agency.

< / p >

< / div >

< div class ="

THE HISTORY

">

< p >

The Himba People are tall, slender and statuesque herders, who live a life of structure in Northern Namibia. The Himba People live in relatively isolated communities, however, they manage to survive and keep their traditions, despite the harsh desert conditions that they live in.

< / p >



< p >

Hairstyles are an important aspect to the Himba people and signify social status and beauty. The women cover their hair and bodies in a red paste that is made out of ochre mixture called “Orcha,” which they believe protects them from the sun (Popovic; 2012).

< / p >

< / div >

< div class ="

HIMBA AGENCY

">

< p >

The Himba people were essentially used as inspiration for our agency, because they have faced many difficulties such as war, genocide and droughts. Through harsh environments and social war, they have survived and kept their traditions and structure of life. As an agency, we believe that no matter what difficulty we face, we will overcome this and come out on top, keeping our values, mission and vision front of mind.

< / p >
