THE BRIEF

">

< p >

Lauren Glover, an honors student at Vega, was doing her thesis on “The Factors Contributing to the Success of the ‘Oh So Heavenly’ Brand”. She asked me to create a mock-up eCommerce website based on her research findings and insights.

< / p >

< / div >

< div class ="

INSIGHTS AND THE CONCEPT

">

< p >

Lauren identified 3 main insights:
1. Consumers named the way in which the Oh So Heavenly products are packaged and promoted at specific occasions during the year as the main reason for purchase.

2. More than half of the Oh So Heavenly consumers became aware of the brand through receiving it as a gift and felt that they then returned to purchase more of the brands products.

3. Consumers felt that they were not purchasing a ‘house brand’ making the product look of less quality, nor were they purchasing a ‘premium brand’ that they were not able to afford. They felt that the brand was meeting them half way in terms of price and quality.

< / p >

< p >

From these insights the concept of “Package me Perfect” was created. The idea is that customers can make up their own 'baskets' of Oh So Heavely products for occassion such as birthdays or Christmas rather than having to buy premade packs.

< / p >

< / div >

< div class ="

EXECUTION

">

< p >

Careful consideration was taken with the design to ensure that the eCommerce website extension matched the brand identity and aesthetics of Oh So Heavenly. The website follows the stucture of most eCommerce sites to ensure ease of use for the client, while still being bright and pretty. Links to their Facebook page and Oh So Heavenly 'Friendship Club' were also included to encourage the formation of a community around the brand.
