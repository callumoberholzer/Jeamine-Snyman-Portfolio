THE BRIEF

">

< p >

Fig Tree Coffee Café is a new coffee shop that has been established in connection with 'Grace Family Church', Riverside. They are a hipster-styled café who are focused on minimalism and simplicity. The café itself is purely white, black and a light coloured wood. As Fig Tree Coffee is still new they are in the process of building an online presence which is why they requested the design of a website.

< / p >

< / div >

< div class ="

INSIGHTS & EXECUTION

">

< p >

Two main insights were identified: firstly as a brand they are focused on community. Secondly they provided a space for moms to enjoy a good cup of coffee while still being able to keep an eye on their children; a benefit few other coffee shops in the area provide.

< / p >



< p >

The design of this website focuses on the sense of community that Fig Tree wants to create. The website becomes a place that cultivates the coffee culture that is so prevalent in South Africa.

< / p >



< p >

Along the back wall of the coffee shop is an aerial shot of Durban. This is Fig Tree's main feature along with the iconic fig tree that grows outside the building. Thus I incorporated that same photograph of Durban into the design of the site. In the website the photograph sits as a fixed background to mimic the wall in the actual coffee shop. The website is clean and minimalistic to match the brand's identity.
